- **Rich MP4s under `apps/` (#3):** The 5 GUI rich renders exist only
  under `docs-site/public/gui-journeys/<id>/`. The source recordings
  still live behind TCC (Accessibility + Screen Recording) grants, so
  `apps/macos/HwLedgerUITests/build/…` never produced a raw
  `recording.mp4` to feed `hwledger-journey-render one`. This is
  consistent with the v1 caveat and `GUI_CAPTURE_PENDING.md`.
- **`apps/cli-journeys/scripts/record-all.sh` (#23):** 36 lines vs the
  ≤5-line target. The extra lines are a binary-existence guard and a
  `hwledger-cli → hwledger` symlink pin required by the VHS tapes.
  The stub header cites the policy doc and defers "real" logic to the
  Rust binary, which meets the policy intent.
- **Journey index linking (#53):** The CLI sub-sections
  (Installation & Setup, Model Ingestion, Releases & Quality) describe
  journeys by name but do not link to the corresponding pages. Tracked
  in the gap backlog.
